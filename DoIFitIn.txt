Do I Fit In?
	- Do not let sterotypes fool you
	- You do not need to be a genius
	- You do not need to be great at math
	- All programmers have some level of imposter syndrome


Education:
	- College is great but not needed.
	- Bootcamps are great but not needed.


WHAT helped me succeed? 
	- PASSION, TIME & THE INTERNET



Do you have passion?
	- You need to LOVE what you do.
	- Wake up excited to write code and learn something new.
	- Feel acomplished when you build something
	- If you hare learning, you are in the WRONG profession!
